Edith King       King, Edith [1871-1962]. Poet born in South Africa, lived primarily in England.--Useful things p. 33
I'd like so very much to have
Some of the useful things
That lucky birds & beasts have got,
And first of all their wings;
For then into the apple-tree
I should not need to climb,
And graze my legs & tear my frock,
In getting down each time.
Then when the flies are troublesome
I'd like to have a tail,
And when I'm battling with the wasps
The beetle's coat of mail.
