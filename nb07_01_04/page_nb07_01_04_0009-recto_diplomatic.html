Leslie Nelson Jennings.       Jennings, Leslie Nelson [1892-1972]. American poet and editor.
Bring me an herb of healing, bring me fruit
Cool from the hills, a charger heaped w snow;
Sea water chill & colorless and mute
In jars of smoky agate; let me know
Rigors of bronze, & all the frozen, white
Procession of lads & girls who stand
Stricken upon the threshold of delight,
Their limbs unloosened hand apart from hand!
These I will put between me and that hour
when Beauty learns to plague the eyes awake       Perhaps anticipating MM's descriptions of beauty in paradise, in "Marriage"; Adam is "plagued" by some aspects.
