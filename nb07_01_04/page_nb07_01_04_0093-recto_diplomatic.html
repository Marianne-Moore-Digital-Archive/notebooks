Ill London News Aug 11 1923       "A Summer 'Seaside' Visitor to be Avoided." Photographs by Dr. Francis Ward. Illustrated London News, 11 September 1923: 21. 45       MM takes notes toward drafting "An Octopus," first published Dial December 1924. Her Notes to the poem refer to this article and claim to quote from Ward.
Finish of a swim–out of breath–
tiptoeing after a swim–Showing the white
under surfaces of the arms. Swimming backward
arms curled up & one used as a leg creeping
towards prey. (The arms seemed to approach
the prey from all directions The lobster
turned over & lay on his back w legs upward.)
eledone cirrosa
cephalopod w single row of suckers on each of arms.
To the uninitiated the ill. would suggest that
it is the cephalopod is looking to the left,
but this is not so. The animal is facing right.
8 arms long tapering cones all joined
at their bases to form a crown around the mouth
