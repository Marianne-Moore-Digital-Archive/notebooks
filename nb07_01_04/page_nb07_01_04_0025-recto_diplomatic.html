King, Edith. "Useful Things" (see previous note).When I am bathing in the sea
And find it hard to float, 11
I'd like to borrow from the duck
Her web & oily coat.
For thus provided I could live
On land in air or sea
And fly and flap & fight & float,
Just as it suited me.
May 217. 1923 Punch. 25 April 1923
