R Southey       Southey, Robert [1774-1843]. English poet, associated with Romanticism. MM pastes in and annotates a newspaper clipping from 1916, containing two of Southey's poems.                                              NY. Times May 7) 1916       MM cancels "nearer as she gazed" in the penultimate line and "tremblingly" in the final line of "The Locust"; she draws parentheses around "Sea"  in the title of "The Sea Anemone" and numbers words in its first line to change their order; she underlines the penultimate and cancels the final line of this poem.

Letter to the editor defending the continued value of Southey's poetry in 1916 and the view that he is still appreciated by some discriminating readers.
