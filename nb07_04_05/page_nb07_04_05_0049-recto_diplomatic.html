He interviewed Sir Kenneth Clark (1903-1983) on his WNET television show Personal Report, on 17 May 1963 [sic]. The NY Times Television guide describes Clark as "author, art historian and chairman of the "Independent Television Authority of Great Britain" (NYT p. 51). MM cites Clark's book Leonardo da Vinci: An Account of His Development as an Artist in her notes to this poem, and refers to him as an "ideal expositor" in "Poetry and Criticism," first published as Poetry and Criticism: Response to Questions Posed by Howard Nemerov.
