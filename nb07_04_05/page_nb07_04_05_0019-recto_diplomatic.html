"Foremost of the Peach Orchard"       Title awarded to female impersonator and actor Mei Lan-Fang [1894-1962].                                                                                                                               13
                                                                   NYT Aug 9 1961       "Stage Actor, Dies: Top Chinese Star Was Noted Female Impersonator."New York Times (9 August 1961): 33. Obituary for Mei Lan-Fang, 67. The piece clarifies that the title "Foremost of the Peach Orchard" indicated that Mei was "without a peer as an actor in China." The actor emerged from retirement after WWII and became a deputy of the National People's Congress in 1955; he did not join the Communist Party until 1959.
